### PROMPT 07: Franchise Operations Manual (Sổ tay Vận hành Nhượng quyền)

#### Mô tả
Sổ tay vận hành toàn diện cho franchisee — "bible" hướng dẫn mọi thứ từ setup, daily operations, staffing, marketing local, đến financial reporting. Đây là "clone" của SOP package (Tầng 1-4) được customize cho franchise context.

#### Thông tin cần thu thập (hỏi người dùng trước khi tạo)
1. SOP package đã đầy đủ chưa? (reference skill 00-12)
2. Franchisee cần tuân thủ 100% hay có phần customize?
3. Brand guidelines: logo, color, signage, uniform — chi tiết?
4. Supply chain: franchisee mua hàng từ đâu? (central / approved vendors / tự do)
5. Technology: POS, CRM, reporting — franchisee dùng hệ thống gì?
6. Compliance: inspections, audits, mystery shopping — tần suất?

#### Template gợi ý
Cấu trúc MAN:
- **Chương 1** — Welcome & Brand: Lịch sử, mission, values, brand promise
- **Chương 2** — Pre-Opening: Site selection, build-out, hiring, training, soft opening
- **Chương 3** — Daily Operations: Opening/closing procedures, service standards, quality control
- **Chương 4** — Staffing: Hiring, training, scheduling, performance management
- **Chương 5** — Products/Services: Menu/catalog, preparation standards, quality specs
- **Chương 6** — Customer Experience: Service standards, complaint handling, loyalty programs
- **Chương 7** — Marketing: Local marketing guidelines, approved materials, social media
- **Chương 8** — Financial Management: Bookkeeping, reporting to franchisor, royalty payment
- **Chương 9** — Technology: POS setup, CRM, reporting dashboard
- **Chương 10** — Compliance: Standards, inspections, corrective actions
- **Chương 11** — Emergency Procedures: Crisis management, PR, insurance
- **Phụ lục**: Checklists per chapter, Contact directory, FAQs

Confirm cấu trúc trước khi tạo.

#### Prompt tạo file
```
Tạo Franchise Operations Manual (Table of Contents + key chapter outlines).

CONTEXT:
- DN: [Tên] — Ngành: [ngành]
- Customization: [100% tuân thủ / phần customize]
- Supply chain: [central / approved vendors / tự do]
- Technology: [POS / CRM / Reporting tools]
- Compliance: [inspection frequency]

FORMAT:
- Table of Contents: 11 chương + phụ lục — numbered sections
- Per chapter: Outline 5-10 sections × key content summary
- Chương 3 (Daily Ops) chi tiết nhất: Morning checklist ☐ | Service flow | Closing checklist ☐
- Chương 8 (Financial): Reporting requirements — what | when | how | to whom
- Brand guidelines section: Logo do's/don'ts | Color codes | Signage specs | Uniform
- Compliance scorecard: Category | Standard | Score (1-5) | Weight | Weighted score → Total
- Quick reference cards: 1-page summaries for daily use per chapter
- Index: Alphabetical topic index for quick lookup

TONE: Clear, prescriptive, franchisee-friendly — "how-to" not "what-to".
LENGTH: 10-15 trang A4 (outline + key chapters; full manual sẽ 50-100+ trang).
```
